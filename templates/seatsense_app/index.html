About SeatSense

SeatSense is a web platform designed to simplify event seat booking. Users can explore upcoming events hosted in our auditoriums, select their preferred seats, and confirm bookings instantly through an interactive seat selection system.

The platform provides a seamless booking experience where users can browse events, view event details, choose seats visually, and manage their bookings through their personal dashboard. SeatSense aims to make event participation easier and more organized.
